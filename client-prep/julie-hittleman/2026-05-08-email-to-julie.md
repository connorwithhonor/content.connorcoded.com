## Optional: turn the prompt into your own Custom GPT (~10 min, one time)

If you want to skip pasting the big prompt every time, you can save it as a **Custom GPT** inside ChatGPT. You only do this once and then it's a single click forever after. You need a paid ChatGPT account, which you already have.

1. Go to **chatgpt.com**, click your name (bottom-left) → **My GPTs** → **Create a GPT**
2. Click the **Configure** tab at the top (skip the "Create" chat builder — it's slower)
3. **Name**: *Julie's Blog Engine* (or whatever you want)
4. **Description**: *Turns a Substack post into a fully optimized 2,500-word blog post in my voice.*
5. **Instructions**: paste **the entire prompt above** (everything between the quote marks), but delete the last two lines that say "Title:" and "Article:" — those are what you'll paste in fresh each time
6. **Conversation starters**: add one that says *"I have a Substack post to convert — here it is:"*
7. **Capabilities**: leave Web Search and Image Generation checked, turn off Code Interpreter
8. Top-right: **Save** → **Only me** → **Confirm**

From then on, every time you want a new post: open ChatGPT → click *Julie's Blog Engine* in your sidebar → paste the title and article → done.

Same idea works for a second GPT just for cover images if you want — same steps, but the instructions are the cover-image prompt above.

## How to Post in Honor Elevate (10 steps)

1. **Sites → Blogs → Hittleman Health Blog → New Post**
2. **Paste the article body** in. Delete the placeholder text that's already there.
3. **Paste the headline** in the title field at top. Make sure there's no stray space before the first letter.
4. Click **Continue** (blue button, top-right)
5. **URL slug**: paste the headline, lowercase, with hyphens — e.g. `why-belly-fat-after-menopause-feels-so-stubborn`
6. **Category**: select **Women Over 40** (we'll add more categories as you grow)
7. **Cover image**: upload the image → paste the alt text from ChatGPT → **Insert media**
8. **Keywords**: 3 to 5, one at a time, **hit Enter after each** (no commas, no hashtags)
9. **Post description**: paste the 100-character description
10. **Author**: select Julie Hittleman → **Publish**

To preview: Blogs page → click the **⋯** on the blog row → **Preview**.
