## Episode Description — Plaintext

The real estate world looks very different than it did when I started selling
homes in 1998. The 2024 commission-related lawsuits forced the industry to
finally be upfront about something agents already knew: commissions are
negotiable — on BOTH sides of the deal.

In this episode of Sellers Only Agent™, I cover:

• What actually shifted after the 2024 commission lawsuits
• The difference between a "flashy" agent and one who can get the job done
• Whether you can buy or sell a home on your own (FSBO / DIY) — and what
  you give up when you do
• California's 42+ seller disclosures and why it's one of the most
  document-heavy real estate markets in the country
• The single best protection move in any transaction: follow up every
  verbal conversation with an email
• A pro move almost no buyer makes: door-knock the neighbors who share a
  property line BEFORE you close escrow

I'm a sellers-only agent in California. If you're thinking about
selling, that's what I do — all day, every day.
