## YouTube Shorts hooks (3 — use 30–45 sec each)

**1. "The flashy agent test"**
"If you're hiring a real estate agent and their entire feed is luxury cars and vacation reels — that's not the agent. That's the highlight reel. The agent worth the commission posts content that protects the consumer, not content that promotes the agent."

**2. "Door-knock the neighbors"**
"Before you close on a house, do this one thing nobody does: door-knock the neighbors on either side. Find out about the barking dog, the leaning fence, the parties. You'll learn more in 10 minutes than you will in the entire disclosure packet. And if your agent won't walk over there with you — that tells you something too."

**3. "Email the conversation"**
"Single best protection move in any real estate deal: after you talk to your agent, your lender, your escrow officer — send a short email. 'Just confirming we talked about X.' That email is the difference between he-said/she-said and a documented record if anything ever goes sideways."
