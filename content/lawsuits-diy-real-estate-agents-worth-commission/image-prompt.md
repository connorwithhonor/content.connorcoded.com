# Image Prompt — "Lawsuits, DIY Real Estate & Is Your Agent Worth the Commission?"

## Primary prompt (16:9 — YouTube thumbnail / blog hero)

> Cinematic, photoreal editorial photograph. A weathered wooden judge's
> gavel resting on a thick stack of real-estate disclosure paperwork on a
> warm-toned desk in the foreground; the paperwork is slightly fanned out
> so the eye reads "documents" without showing any legible words. In the
> soft-focus background, a classic Southern California single-story
> stucco home with a clay-tile roof, a "For Sale" yard sign turned
> slightly so its lettering is unreadable, mature palm trees, golden-hour
> sunlight casting long shadows across a manicured lawn. Composition uses
> shallow depth of field, 50mm lens look, balanced rule-of-thirds, warm
> cinematic color grade (amber + teal), subtle film grain. Mood:
> trustworthy, serious, protective, professional — not flashy. 16:9
> aspect ratio, ultra-detailed, 8K, editorial real-estate magazine cover
> energy.

**Negative prompt:** text, letters, words, watermarks, logos, captions,
distorted hands, deformed faces, cartoon, illustration, low quality,
oversaturated, neon, lens flare overload, garish colors, signage that's
readable, AI-typical hallucinated text.

## Vertical variant (9:16 — Shorts / Reels / Stories)

> Same scene, recomposed vertically: gavel and disclosure stack lower-third,
> California home centered upper two-thirds with golden-hour sky and a
> single palm silhouette. Maintain shallow depth of field and cinematic
> warm color grade. 9:16 aspect ratio, no text, no readable signage.

## Square variant (1:1 — Buzzsprout episode art / Instagram feed)

> Same scene tightened to a 1:1 frame: gavel resting at a 30° angle on
> stacked disclosure paperwork, California stucco home softly blurred
> behind, warm golden-hour light. Centered composition, no text in image.
